SPINDCD Privacy Policy

At SPINDCD, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users of the SPINDCD application. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use our app. Any information entered into the app, such as options for the "Spin To Decide" game, is used solely within the context of the app and is not stored or transmitted to any external servers.

2. Data Storage

SPINDCD does not store or retain any user data on our servers or any third-party servers. All data entered into the app by users is processed locally on the user's device and is not shared with any external entities.

3. Permissions

SPINDCD does not require any special permissions to function. The app does not access any personal data on your device, including but not limited to contacts, location, or media files.

4. Advertising

We do not display any third-party advertisements within the SPINDCD application. Our app is free from ad networks and ad tracking mechanisms.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to the policy will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the practices of the SPINDCD application, please contact us at contact@spindcd.com.
